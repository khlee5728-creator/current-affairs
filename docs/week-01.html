Grammar

수동태 (Passive Voice)

수동태는 주어가 동작의 대상이 되는 문장 구조입니다. 형태: be동사 + 과거분사

Rome was not built in a day.
로마는 하루아침에 건설되지 않았다.
동작(build)의 주체를 특정하기가 어려워 목적어(Rome)를 주어로 → 수동태
English is spoken all over the world.
영어는 전 세계에서 쓰인다.
More than 96% of the world's annual rice crop is produced in the Far East.
Investigators discovered that a window had been broken from the outside.
The event will be held at the stadium.
A mistake has been made.

수동태가 불가능한 경우: 자동사(1형식)

자동사(1형식)는 수동태로 전환할 수 없습니다.

The hotel stands on the beach. (O)
The accident happened close to the junction.
"was happened" (X) — happen은 자동사이므로 수동태 불가
The metal frame remained after the fire.
"was remained" (X)
Then he disappeared behind the crowd.
"was disappeared" (X)
The group consists of a comedian, singers and actors.
"is consisted of" (X)
The blue coat belongs to her.
"is belonged to" (X)
He seems very quiet.
"is seemed" (X)

타동사이나 수동태 불가

일부 타동사는 의미상 자동동사(2형식)처럼 쓰여 수동태가 불가능합니다.

He resembles his father.
"is resembled" (X) — 의미상으로 자동동사(2형식)
This color really becomes you.
"is become" (X)
