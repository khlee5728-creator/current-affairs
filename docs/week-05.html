046 부정부패 ▼
AThe scandal once again showed how rampant various kinds of corruption are in our society.
BWhen will we be able to live in a transparent society free of corruption?
Simple
There are many kinds of corruption in our society.
Upgrade
Various kinds of corruption are rampant in our society.
048 부정부패 ▼
ADo you think the newly-enacted anti-corruption law will really eradicate the corruption in our society?
BIt won't be easy to root out the decades-long problem overnight, but the law is really far-reaching, so it will at least do something.
Simple
The law will have a huge influence.
Upgrade
The law is really far-reaching.
049 부정부패 ▼

Upgrade
Politicians take corporate payouts under the table.
050 부정부패 ▼

Don't let a few bad apples ruin your perception of the whole police force.

058 남북관계, 북한 ▼

Simple

Upgrade
Any form of reunification that puts the four superpowers at odds would be unthinkable.
060 남북관계, 북한 ▼
ADo you think we should reunify with North Korea and live as one nation?

Simple

Upgrade
I think we are better off now than~

061 남북관계, 북한 ▼

Upgrade

062 남북관계, 북한 ▼
ASome North Korean defectors fail to adapt to our society and live in poverty or even become criminals.
BThe government should help them assimilate into our society, and the defectors themselves also have to do their best to integrate into our society.
Simple

Upgrade
The government should help them assimilate into our society.
063 남북관계, 북한 ▼
AThe primary role of helping these defectors become full-fledged members of society should be played by the government.
BThe public also has to welcome them into our society.
Simple
North Korean defectors should become complete members of society.
Upgrade
North Korea defectors should become full-fledged members of society.
* full-fledged: 완전한 자격을 갖춘 / *fledge: 깃털

Grammar

품사와 문장구조

영어 문장을 이해하려면 문장성분과 품사의 관계를 파악하는 것이 중요합니다.

1. 문장성분 5가지

영어 문장은 주어(Subject), 동사(Verb), 목적어(Object), 보어(Complement), 수식어(Modifier) 5가지 성분으로 구성됩니다.

2. 필수 문장성분과 5형식

영어 문장은 동사의 종류에 따라 5가지 형식으로 나뉩니다.

1형식: S + V
주어 + 동사
2형식: S + V + C
주어 + 동사 + 보어
3형식: S + V + O
주어 + 동사 + 목적어
4형식: S + V + IO + DO
주어 + 동사 + 간접목적어 + 직접목적어
5형식: S + V + O + C
주어 + 동사 + 목적어 + 보어

3. 8품사

영어에는 명사, 대명사, 동사, 형용사, 부사, 전치사, 접속사, 감탄사의 8가지 품사가 있습니다.

4. 문장성분과 품사의 관계

각 문장성분 자리에 올 수 있는 품사가 정해져 있습니다.

주어 자리 → 명사(대명사)
Subject position takes nouns or pronouns
동사 자리 → 동사
Verb position takes verbs
목적어 자리 → 명사(대명사)
Object position takes nouns or pronouns
보어 자리 → 명사(대명사), 형용사
Complement position takes nouns, pronouns, or adjectives
수식어 → 부사, 형용사
Modifier position takes adverbs or adjectives

5. 품사를 알면 문장구조를 파악할 수 있음

단어의 뜻뿐 아니라 품사까지 함께 알아야 문장 이해가 가능합니다.
