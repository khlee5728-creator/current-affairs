Key Expressions

문장성분 Subject (S) Verb (V) Object (O) Complement (C) Modifier (M)
004 복지 ▼
AThe government should provide free school lunches to public school students across the board.
BI don't agree with that idea. Free school lunches should be selectively provided only to students from low income families, rather than across the board.
Simple
Free school lunches should be indiscriminately provided to all the students.
Upgrade
Free school lunches should be provided to all the students across the board.
005 복지 ▼
AI support the new government's tax cut plans. They will help the poor through a trickle-down effect.
BThere 's no empirical evidence that tax cuts actually benefit the poor.
Simple
The tax cut will help the poor through spreading wealth from the rich to the poor.
Upgrade
The tax cut will help the poor through a trickle-down effect.
* the + poor 형용사의 명사화 / the + rich 형용사의 명사화
006 복지 ▼
AThere needs to be some way to prevent people from abusing our welfare system.
BThere are individuals who leech on them. They take away the benefits from those who are really in need.
Simple
We should prevent people from illegally taking advantage of our welfare system.
Upgrade
We should prevent people from abusing our welfare system.
007 빈부격차 ▼
AThe disparity between the rich and the poor has been increasing over the past decade.
BI know. The real problem is we don't have any powerful countermeasures in our hands.
Simple
Rich people have become richer while poor people have become poorer.
Upgrade
The disparity between the rich and poor has been increasing.
* disparity between = gap between
008 빈부격차 ▼
AWhile Korea's conglomerates enjoy the record profits, the middle class is being wiped out.
BI don't know when this problem of economic polarization will ever be resolved.
Simple
The middle class disappears.
Upgrade
The middle class is being wiped out.
009 빈부격차 ▼
AIt 's difficult to live on the current minimum wage. One cannot maintain a livelihood with the present amount.
BBut a sharp increase in the amount would ultimately be a blow to small business owners.
Simple
It's difficult to make a living with the current minimum wage.
Upgrade
It's difficult to live on the current minimum wage.
010 물가 ▼
AI went to a supermarket today and almost had a price shock.
BThe economy is slow, but prices for groceries and basic necessities are rising.
Simple
I was surprised to see how much prices have increased.
Upgrade
I had a price shock.
* price shock = sticker shock
011 물가 ▼
AI don't understand how these stores can be allowed to make their products so expensive.
BIf there were stronger regulations on price gouging, we wouldn't have this problem.
Simple
We need stronger regulations on unfair price increase.
Upgrade
We need stronger regulations on price gouging.
012 물가 ▼
AMy energy bills have almost doubled in amount since last year.
BWe have a right to basic necessities, so these companies are completely immoral for that.
Simple
We have a right to consume what is necessary for a living.
Upgrade
We have a right to basic necessities.
* basic necessities = bare necessities

Grammar

수동태 (Passive Voice) - continued

수동태 형태: be + 과거분사. 이번 주 표현에서 나타나는 수동태 예시를 살펴봅니다.

Free school lunches should be selectively provided only to students from low income families.
Free school lunches should be indiscriminately provided to all the students.
Free school lunches should be provided to all the students across the board.

능동태를 수동태로 변환

수동태에서 by + 행위의 주체를 생략하는 경우가 많습니다.

능동: You have made a mistake.
수동: A mistake has been made.
행위의 주체(you)가 일반적이므로 by you를 생략
