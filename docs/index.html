Instructor: Kyunghee Lee Tues / Thurs 18:30~21:30 Online Zoom Class

Course Introduction

In this course, through reading and discussing English materials related to domestic and international issues, we aim to improve practical English proficiency and cultivate critical thinking skills. Furthermore, we will explore various cultures related to the learning topics, providing an opportunity to develop a balanced attitude towards other cultures.

Learning Objectives

1Students can acquire domestic and international current affairs information and knowledge.
2Students can improve English reading comprehension and expressive skills.
3Students can experience and understand the culture of different countries indirectly.
4Students can formulate their own opinion regarding controversial current issues.
