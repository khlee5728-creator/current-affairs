### 📜 Course Introduction

In this course, through reading and discussing English materials related to domestic and international issues, we aim to improve practical English proficiency and cultivate critical thinking skills. Furthermore, we will explore various cultures related to the learning topics, providing an opportunity to develop a balanced attitude towards other cultures.

### 🗝 Learning Objectives

|  | **Learning Objectives** | **Assessments** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Students can acquire domestic and international current affairs information and knowledge. | exam, homework, presentation and participation |
| 2 | Students can improve English reading comprehension and expressive skills. | exam, homework, presentation and participation |
| 3 | Students can experience and understand the culture of different countries indirectly. | exam, homework, presentation and participation |
| 4 | Students can formulate their own opinion regarding the controversial current issue being debated. | exam, homework, presentation and participation |

### 📚 Textbooks

Learning materials (ppt, pdf, html files) will be provided every class.

### 📝 Assignment

Students spend approximately 30 minutes during class finding and reading articles related to the topic. They then write a summary of the article and present it.
